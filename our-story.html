OUR STORY

Why Qevilo exists.

Enterprise rigor, at the agile scale of a small business.

Qevilo started with a simple, slightly annoyed observation: too many good small businesses were being held back by things that had nothing to do with the quality of their work — a booking system that only worked over the phone, a website built a decade ago, spreadsheets standing in for a CRM.

We're a small Berlin-based team pairing two disciplines that rarely sit in the same room: technical engineering and automated testing on one side, UX/UI design and brand positioning on the other. Most agencies pick one. We built Qevilo because small businesses need both — and shouldn't have to hire two agencies, and referee between them, to get it.

We treat your administrative, technical, and design challenges as our own, so you can focus completely on your core expertise. That's the whole premise. Technology should support your business, never complicate it.
